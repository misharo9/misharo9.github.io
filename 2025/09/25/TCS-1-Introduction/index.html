TCS 1 : Introduction
Sep 25, 2025

Theoretical computer science is a discipline which studies about computation theory. It tries to answer the following question :

  1. What is computation?
  2. What are the problems suitable for computers to solve? Why are some problems impossible for computers to solve?
  3. What is the mysterious connection between complexity theory and cryptography?

In this series of blogs, I will introduce some basic concepts and fun facts in TCS, and hopefully you will be guided through the intellectual landscape of Turing Machine, lambda-calculus, complexity theory, and cryptography.

Prerequisites

You should have some basic knowledge about :

  1. Mathematical logic, especially about propositional calculus and ZFC.
  2. Graph theory.
  3. Turing machine.
